About Back2Nature Wellness Market

Back2Nature Wellness Market is a carefully curated monthly gathering celebrating natural health, beauty, and wellness. Founded by Herbal Artista, our mission is to connect makers, practitioners, and the community in Alameda for hands-on experiences, education, and genuine engagement.


This is a vibrant space that offers artisans and wellness experts showcase their products, share knowledge, and help visitors discover a healthier, more mindful lifestyle. From herbal remedies to handmade body care, every booth is thoughtfully selected to ensure quality and authenticity.


Whether you are a vendor looking to grow your business or a visitor seeking inspiration, Back2Nature Wellness Market is your monthly destination for wellness, creativity, and community connection.
